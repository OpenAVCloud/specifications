# Purpose

This document outlines the taxonomy guidelines for Audio/Video (AV)
devices developed, deployed, or integrated by members of the OpenAVCloud
initiative.

The goal of this document is to capture all the possible categories of
AV products that can be integrated and managed at a customer site. The
intent will be to use these categories to define ontologies and data
models as well as more detailed APIs for the functionality they provide,
in a later document published by the OpenAVCloud technical working
group.

# High-Level Device Categories

Below is a taxonomy of AV (Audio-Visual) devices organized into
categories based on their function, usage context, and technical
characteristics:

#  Display Devices

Devices that present visual content to users.

# Monitors & Screens 

Examples of these may include LCD/LED Monitors, OLED Displays,
Touchscreens, and Passive Screens.

#  Projectors 

Examples of these may include DLP Projectors, LCD Projectors, and Laser
Projectors.

# Video Walls 

Examples of these may include LED Panels, AIO LED Walls, and LCD Video
Walls.

# Interactive Whiteboards 

Examples of these may include Smart Boards, and Digital Flipcharts.

#  Audio Devices

Devices that capture, process, or reproduce sound.

# Microphones 

Examples of these may include Dynamic Microphones, Condenser
Microphones, Lavalier Microphones, Boundary Microphones, and Beamforming
Microphones.

# Speakers 

Examples of these may include Passive Speakers, Active Speakers, Ceiling
Speakers and Soundbars.

# Amplifiers & Mixers 

Examples of these may include Audio Mixers, Power Amplifiers and Digital
Signal Processors (DSPs).

#  Video Devices

Devices that capture or transmit video.

# Cameras 

Examples of these may include PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Cameras, Fixed
Cameras, USB Cameras, and Document Cameras.

# Video Capture & Streaming Devices 

Examples of these may include Capture Cards, and Streaming Encoders.

# Codec-based Systems

#  Control Systems

Devices and software used to manage AV systems.

# Control Panels 

Examples of these may include Touch Panels, and Button Panels.

# Remote Controls 

Examples of these may include IR Remotes, and RF Remotes.

# Automation Systems 

Examples of these may include Room Scheduling Panels, and Environmental
Controls (lighting, shades).

Examples of these may include also include software bases AV control
systems such as On-Prem Control Systems, and Cloud-based AV Management
Platforms.

#  Signal Management & Distribution

Devices that manage AV signal routing and processing.

# Switchers & Matrix Switches 

Examples of these may include HDMI/SDI/AV Switchers, and Video Matrix
Routers.

# Extenders & Converters 

Examples of these may include HDMI over IP, HDBaseT, Fiber Optic
Extenders, Format Converters (e.g., VGA to HDMI), and DisplayPort/USB-C
DP Alt-mode devices.

# Splitters & Distribution Amplifiers 

Examples of these may include Audio Splitters, and Video Distribution
Amplifiers.

#  Collaboration & Video Conferencing Systems

Devices and platforms that facilitate interaction and teamwork.

# Interactive Displays

These may also include Digital Whiteboarding Tools

# Wireless Presentation Systems 

These may also include Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) and Bring Your Own
Meeting (BYOM) systems.

# Unified Communications Platforms 

These may also include Integrated Room Systems.

#  Recording & Playback Devices

Devices used for capturing and replaying AV content.

# Media Players 

Examples of these may include Blu-ray/DVD Players, and Streaming Devices
(Apple TV, Chromecast).

# Recorders 

Examples of these may include Digital Video Recorders (DVRs), and
Network Video Recorders (NVRs).

# Storage Solutions 

Examples of these may include NAS Devices, Cloud Storage Platforms.

#  Infrastructure & Accessories

Supporting components for AV systems.

#  Power Management 

Examples of these may include UPS Systems, Power Conditioners, and Power
Distribution Units (PDU).

# Networking Equipment 

Examples of these may include Switches, Routers, Wireless Access Points,
and Network Gateways.
